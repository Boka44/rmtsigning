Loan Signings

The signing process is the most nervous part of the real estate process. As a NNA Certified Notary Signing Agent (NSA) or Loan Signing Agent (LSA) we are specifically trained to facilitate real estate loan transactions. These specialized California Notary Publics have extensive training and experience in the execution of loan documents.

The task of the NSA is vital to the successful closing and funding in a real estate transaction. Mortgage lenders, title companies, escrow companies, and everyone involved in the real estate transaction rely heavily on the competence of the NSA to complete the singing and notatarize portions of the real estate loan documents in a professional, reliable, and accurate manner.

We walk borrowers through all the important documents including the closing statement, note, deed of trust, and closing disclosures. We help make sure that all forms come back completely filled out, helping illuminate any funding conditions and frustrations through the closing process.
